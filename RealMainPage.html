Borderlands 3 was just recently announced for release, but for PC players it's onlyavailable on the Epic Games Store, and people ARE NOT happy about it. Borderlands 3 is set to release in mid September on Xbox,PS4 and the Epic Games Store. The game will remain on Epic Games Store for 6 months after release.
